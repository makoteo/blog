The story

Why make this?

I thought it was funny. That's it. Admittedly, I didn't expect to put this much effort into it, but I kind of got caught up in all the interesting statistics. Also, I needed a final project for CS50x and thought this could be a simple enough website to create. Originally, it was just supposed to be a 50/50 chance every round, but it became evident quite soon that that was boring, which is why I implemented all the powerups.

Is there any meaning to this?

Nope. I mean, I guess it's a fun social experiment to figure out how long people will spend playing a game which they have no influence over, but that's about it. You can go to a casino if you want to see that.

What is your high score?

As of writing this answer, 11.

Well actually, I just got 12 but I'm too lazy to go and rewrite every reference to the 11, so everyone's going to forever think I never got more than that.

Sources for fun facts and timeline?

The timeline events mostly come from this wikipedia article, which is a very interesting read on it's own. The fun facts for different scores come from various sources, and that's why they may seem quite ridiculous at times. Even I don't believe them.
