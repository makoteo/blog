The Business

Instructions

The instructions are simple. Use WASD or the arrow keys on your keyboard to move around. Using this, collect the money that's appearing from thin air to generate profit.
However, don't drop money, as you will lose the profit it could've given you. And if you lose too much profit, you lose the game. It's simple.
To keep a check over this, the game has two score counters. The one on the left counts your highest balance of the game, the one on your right counts your current balance.
That may seem complicated, and so the game has a bar under your player. All you have to remember is that while there's no red on it, you're okay.
Now go ahead, have fun! And remember, the more money falls, the harder it gets.
